About Us

Welcome to Arihant's

Your Premier Destination for Exceptional Textile and Linen Solutions!

Arihant's stands as a distinguished textile brand nestled in the heart of Paschim Bardhaman, Durgapur, West Bengal. Since our establishment in 1995, we have been the trusted purveyor of top-tier uniforms for a diverse clientele, including schools, offices, hospitals, and various esteemed organizations.

Beyond uniforms, Arihant's extends its expertise to encompass uniform sarees, bedsheets, blankets, and a comprehensive array of textile and linen essentials. With an extensive client roster boasting over 700 discerning organizations, we take immense pride in our unwavering commitment to delivering superlative quality products at prices that remain highly competitive.

At Arihant's, we uphold a steadfast belief in delivering an unparalleled level of service. Our team comprises seasoned professionals who are ever-ready to assist you in discovering the perfect products tailored precisely to your unique requirements.
